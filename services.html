Structural Steel Fabrication and Erection Services

KMBJ Structural Steel, Inc. is a company that fabricates, supplies, erects, and provides FOB services for structural steel, metal decking, wrought steel, and ornamental steel for new construction, tenant improvements, and renovation or remodeling projects. The company fabricates and erects a wide variety of beams and columns, with Wide Flanges and HSS (Hollow Structural Sections), along with steel accessories such as plates, angles, channels, and bars. KMBJ also supplies and installs metal decking for floors, roofs, and canopies, as well as deck accessories as ledgers and bent plates.

The catalog of fabrication and installation services, including shop drawings, is as follows:

    1. Fabrication and installation of columns and beams using HSS and wide flanges:
  • Moment frames
  • Braces
  • Cantilevered beams

      • 2. Installation of metal decking:
  • Metal deck for floors and roofs
  • Metal deck for canopies
  • Accessories for metal decking, including ledgers, bent plates, and braces

      • 3. Fabrication and installation, or FOB, of miscellaneous steel elements such as:
  • MetaL stairs
  • Railings
  • Guardrails
  • Handrails
  • Gates and doors
  • Bollards

      • 4. Special services such as:
  • Strongbacks
  • Retrofits
