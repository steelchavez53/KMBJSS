About KMBJ Structural Steel Company

The company was founded on August 8, 2005, in Los Angeles, California, with the objective of providing solutions in structural steel, wrought iron, and other metal elements for residential and commercial buildings. It is licensed by the LA City Steel Fabricator (#FB03342) and the Contractors State License Board, with California Contractor’s License 1044441 and classifications: C-51 Structural Steel Contractor and C-23 Ornamental Metal Contractor. The official address and headquarters are located at 12929 Telegraph Rd Unit I, Santa Fe Springs, CA 90670.

 

Mission Statement

We are fabricators and erectors of steel for construction and architectural finishes, committed to serving society and our valued clients, with whom we aim to build long-term partnerships. Driven by passion, quality, and continuous improvement, we prioritize the occupational health and safety of our personnel, whom we consider our greatest asset in delivering complete customer satisfaction.

 

Quality Policy

We are committed to delivering high-quality services that ensure complete satisfaction, in full compliance with design requirements, regulations, codes, and the expectations of our clients. To achieve this, we focus on

  • Continuous improvement of our processes while maintaining an effective quality management system.
  • Executing projects with dedication, adhering to technical specifications, standards, codes, and schedules.
  • Regularly communicating this policy to all employees, suppliers, and customers.
