Service Area of the Structural Steel Contractor in California


KMBJ Structural Steel, Inc. is headquartered and has its main fabrication workshop in Los Angeles. From there, the company transports steel structures for on-site erection and installation, as well as ships parts Forward on Board (FOB) across Southern California, from cities like Carlsbad, Escondido, and Indio, to Northern cities like Santa Rosa, Sacramento, and Roseville. The coverage includes the entire eastern and western regions between these cities.
