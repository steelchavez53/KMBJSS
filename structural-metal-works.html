Columns and Beams Fabrication and Erection

The Steel Columns and Beams are architectural and Structural engineering elements of the buildings. The column is an element that transmit trough compression, the weight of the structure above to another structural element below. The beam is a lineal element usually horizontal, and carry vertical loads in its axis to transmit the support points.

According the designer that specify in the plans, some parts, elements and works are fabricated in the shop, to finally be erected in the field or the client place. The columns and beams are designed and fabricated usually with HSS (Hollow Structural Section) or wide flanges. The fabrication and erection of steel columns and beams are demanded mainly by general contractors, land or building owners and tenants; to improve o remodel the buildings.

Reinforcement of Girders, Joists and Trusses

The Steel Girder and the Joist are both types of Beams. A Beam is a general term, that refers to a structural member in a structure, that support a roof, floor or other load above, which is supported by a column or columns. Joist is a Structural member supported by Girders or other Beams.

The steel Girders and Joists are designed by Structural Engineers, and architects for industrial and commercial buildings principally. Sometimes they need to be reinforced to increase their strength to accommodate added loads for which they were not originally designed, also due to maintenance against the deterioration or damages of the structures.

The KMBJ Structural Steel company attends for Erect, Install or Reinforce the Girders and Joist in the counties of Los Angeles, Orange, Ventura, San Bernardino, Riverside and San Diego in Southern California State.

Steel Strong Backs

A Strong Back is a Steel Frame which acts as Secondary Support member in an existing Structure. Is usually designed and placed as frame in a new opening through an existing wall as a new; door, window, or to remove all or part of the wall.

The Strong Backs are required by the general contractors, owners of warehouses, and home owners that needs modify their properties. For this reason, it is only necessary to determine their location and their complements as door, windows, and other frames, to be designed; and finally placed or installed.

The company mainly serves and install the Steel Strong Backs in Los Angeles in Southern California and Surrounding Areas.
